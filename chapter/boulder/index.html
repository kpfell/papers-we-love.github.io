CO : Dapper, a LargeScale Distributed Systems Tracing Infrastructure

Map Date/Time: 2014-04-09 07:00pm Location: Oracle - 1300 Walnut St, Suite 200, Boulder

John Claus will be discussing Google's Dapper paper, a tracing system used by Google for debugging large scale distributed systems.

The people at Oracle have been gracious enough to offer their space for our first meeting.  Feel free to show up from 6 to 6:45 for beer, pizza and socializing.  We'll aim to get started by 6:45, which should give ample time to discuss the paper and leave time at the end for questions.

The Oracle office is on the southeast corner of 13th & Walnut.  Please follow signs pointing towards the east entrance of the building.

If you're driving into Boulder for the event, the Boulder Transit Center offers parking just off 14th & Walnut.  There's also a parking lot across from Oracle, on the northwest corner of 13th & Walnut.
